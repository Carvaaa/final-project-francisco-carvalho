1. Bugatti Chiron Super Sport 300+

Price: $3,900,000

The Bugatti Chiron Super Sport 300+ is on the top of the list not only because of it's luxury and looks, but also because of it's incredible record.

This car is currently the fastest production car ever. Beating the incredible 300mph mark, hence the 300+ in it's name, other cars that claim to go as fast or faster have either never been tested or if they had been they actually haven't gone faster and the run was probably unnoficial and based. This Bugatti is legendary.

Specs:

  • Engine: 8.0L Quad-Turbo W16 Gas
  • Transmission: 7-Speed DSG
  • Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive
  • Horsepower: 1,577hp
  • Top Speed: 304.77 Mph
  • 0-62 mph: 2.4s

2. Lamborghini Centenario

Price: $3,000,000-$6,000,000

This Limited Lamborghini is a masterpiece, both in engineering and artistically. It's body is made entirely of carbon fiber, designed for great speeds and agility. Truly an amazing car by Lamborghini.

Specs:

  • Engine: 6.5L V12 Gas
  • Transmission: 7-Speed ISR semi-automatic
  • Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive
  • Horsepower: 770hp
  • Top Speed: 217 Mph
  • 0-62 mph: 2.9s
